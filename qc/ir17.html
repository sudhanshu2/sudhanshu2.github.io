The Quizzing Club came into existence in 1983 and even after 34 years of existence is among the most prestigious student quizzing organisations in Delhi NCR. The Quizzing Club has won many honours in various quizzes. We are proud to present the second edition of Infinitus Reus. Infinitus Reus is a one day quizzing extravaganza featuring top student quizzers of Delhi NCR from the most prestigious schools of India. It will be held on 12th August 2017. Featuring a general quiz where top quizzers will rack their brains to get the correct answer. Infinitus Reus is a quiz like no other. Featuring top ranked quiz masters, the general quiz will challenge the best of the best.
