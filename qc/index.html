About Us
The Quizzing Club of Delhi Public School, R.K. Puram came into existence in 1983, the first club to come up in the school. Thirty years later, it is still one of the most prestigious clubs/societies of the school. From winning the very first Columban Open Quiz in 1987 to winning the Columban Open Quiz in 2015, the club has had an amazingly great journey. DPS, R.K. Puram's Quizzing Club is still a force to reckon with at quizzes not only at the city level, but at the national level as well. The club was, is and hopefully will remain one of the most successful of Quizzing Societies and Clubs in India.
